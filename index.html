OUR VISION

The vision of this initiative is to make the practice of the craft kantha more feasible, driving a sense of promotion of thr craft amongst the youth in terms of modern Gen-Z ways for an easy approach to the craft along-with levitating the self-confidence and belief of the artisans by rewarding them.

OUR MISSION

The community is an initiative to virtually provide a platform for the students involved in the craft sector and willing to achieve optimum expertise in this craft. Thereby, connecting with the master artisans and renowned Kantha artists to the pursuers over live videos, one-to-one live video chat without visiting the cluster in person from any corner of the world. Thereby, giving a recognition to the master artisans, leaving them to embrace technology and prosper. Besides earning rewards and allowances attained from the projects carried out through the community.
